1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At FlowHabits, we believe your personal data belongs to you. We have designed our app with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use FlowHabits.

2. Data We Collect

FlowHabits does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your habit data is stored locally on your device.

Here's a breakdown of data handling:

Data Type Collected? Where Stored
Habit names & data No Your device only
Personal information No Not applicable
Usage analytics No Not applicable
Location data No Not applicable
Purchase history By Apple Apple's servers

3. Local Storage

All your habit data, including:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Check-in history and streaks
  • Personal goals and settings
  • App preferences

...is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. This data is included in your iCloud backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

FlowHabits uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy.
  • Apple WidgetKit: For home screen widgets. Widget data is processed locally on your device.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase in FlowHabits:

  • All payment processing is handled by Apple
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple may share purchase confirmation with us to unlock premium features

For more information about how Apple handles your payment data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features
  • If your device is protected with a passcode or Face ID/Touch ID, your app data is encrypted
  • We recommend keeping your device software up to date for the best security

7. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Use the "Erase All Data" option in Settings to delete all habit data
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • iCloud backups containing app data can be managed through your iCloud settings

8. Children's Privacy

FlowHabits does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal information to function.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
